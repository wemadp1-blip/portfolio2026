Concept — Metabolic Logic

"Fitness and data infrastructure share the same metabolic logic: both generate heat, require controlled atmosphere, and depend on continuous circulatory flow."

The section does not separate these programs. It forces them into a single structural frame where their shared biology becomes the architecture. The pool sits directly above the data center — water becomes a ceiling, filtering light into the fitness floor while thermally separating it from the server infrastructure below.

Site Mapping

Downtown
LA — 6th Hope

Diagrammatic exploration of spatial flows, density, and site boundaries to inform the building's orientation and massing. The site sits at a major convergence of transit, infrastructure, and pedestrian flow in downtown Los Angeles.

The site analysis radiates outward from the building's footprint — mapping sun angles, pedestrian desire lines, and program adjacencies across the urban block.

Kinetic Inspiration

Motion
into Form

Photographic abstraction of motion and performance used as formal inspiration for the building's tectonics. Athletic movement — the stretch, the pivot, the suspended moment — is translated into architectural gesture.

Beginning with moving sports figures, morphing them into abstract shapes became a method of cutting into one singular cube, forming new three-dimensional shapes from the original 2D silhouettes.

Form Extraction

From 2D
to 3D Mass

Conceptual volumes generated through translation and rotation of movement data into architectural gestures. The massing model begins as a cube — the most neutral container — which is then carved by the profiles of athletes in motion.

Massing

Suspended
at Contact

The building touches down only at certain, thoughtful points — spiking through the equally suspended pool. The slight curves, still representing the inevitable movement of athletes, meet at sharp corners, forming both the massing and the water that flows through it continuously.

Short Film — Interior Views

Three
Atmospheres

Rendered in Unreal Engine 5, the short film moves through three distinct atmospheric zones — the pool surface from below, the data hall at depth, and the catwalk that connects the two programs at mid-section.

Plan Section

Shared
Biology

The plan section shows the full horizontal depth of the building — pools above, data halls below, the building's mechanical core threading through both. The sectional cut makes visible what is usually hidden: the thermal exchange between a body training and a server cooling.

The mechanical core of the data center: cooling systems and server infrastructure occupy the same sectional zone as the pool deck above, making the thermal exchange between programs visible.

Physical Model

Reconfigured
Cube

Starting with a cube with ranging pieces that interlock and slide into each other, to reconfiguring and morphing it into a new, complex massing model. The physical model makes the logic of subtraction tactile — you can see the voids left by athletic silhouettes.
